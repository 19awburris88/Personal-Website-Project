EatHere

WordPress / React

EatHere is a one-stop, social engagement marketplace designed for anyone to discover and purchase from F&B brands. It gathers content from social networks, news publications, and blogs, displaying stories, videos, and user-generated recommendations in a streamlined format. Users can engage with food trends, shop directly, share dining experiences, and earn rewards.

More Smoke

In-Early Development

The More Smoke app is designed to be the ultimate digital hub for cigar enthusiasts, offering a seamless experience for discovering premium cigars, engaging with the community, and managing personal collections. Users can explore a curated selection, read expert reviews, and track personal tasting notes in a sleek, user-friendly interface.
