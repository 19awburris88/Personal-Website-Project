EatHere

Wordpress / React

EatHere is a one-stop, social engagement marketplace designed for anyone to discover and purchase from F&B brands.It gathers content from social networks, news publications and blogs. Then the site displays stories, articles, blog posts, videos and other pieces of content in a discoverable format. Users can save & organize links to content that is aggregated from online and social media. The company allows users to discover the latest food trends, shop directly, upload their own dining suggestions, share with their friends and earn rewards, enabling a platform for micro, small and medium F&B businesses.






More Smoke

In-Early Development

The More Smoke app is designed to be the ultimate digital hub for cigar enthusiasts, providing a seamless experience for discovering premium cigars, engaging with the cigar community, and managing personal cigar collections. Whether you're a seasoned aficionado or a newcomer to the lifestyle, the app offers a curated space to explore a wide selection of cigars, read expert reviews, and track personal tasting notes. With a sleek and user-friendly interface, More Smoke simplifies the cigar experience, ensuring users can easily access detailed information on blends, origins, and pairings, helping them make informed choices for every occasion.
