Building Digital Experiences with Code & Creativity

Austin Burris is the owner of More Smoke, a premium cigar lifestyle brand that celebrates the rich cultural heritage of smoking traditions. With a focus on quality craftsmanship, More Smoke delivers a curated experience for cigar enthusiasts while expanding its reach into lifestyle apparel and accessories.

In addition to running More Smoke, Austin is currently a full-stack development student at Fullstack Academy. With a keen interest in AI and machine learning, he is leveraging his studies to integrate technology into the cigar industry and enhance consumer engagement.

Before launching More Smoke, Austin Burris was the founder of EatHere, a pioneering food-tech brand that grew into a nationwide multimedia platform. Built on WordPress, EatHere connected food lovers with local restaurants, chefs, and culinary experiences through engaging digital content, social media, and strategic partnerships.
